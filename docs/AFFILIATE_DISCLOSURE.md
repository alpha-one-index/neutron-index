# Affiliate Disclosure

**Last updated: March 2025**

## Overview

Some of the links in this repository and on [alphaoneindex.com](https://alphaoneindex.com) are affiliate links. This means that when you click on these links and sign up for or purchase services, we may earn a commission at **no additional cost to you**.

These commissions help support the ongoing development, maintenance, and hosting of this open-source project.

## How Affiliate Links Work

When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase or sign up for a service, the provider pays us a small referral fee. This does not increase the price you pay. The provider pricing is the same whether you use our link or go directly to their website.

## Editorial Independence

Our affiliate relationships do **not** influence:
- Which providers are included in our dataset
- The pricing data we collect and publish
- Our NeutronScore / OracleScore composite ratings
- The order in which providers appear in our data
- Any benchmark results or performance comparisons

All data in this repository is collected programmatically from public APIs and official pricing pages. Our methodology is fully documented in [docs/methodology.md](methodology.md) and all collection source code is open.

## FTC Compliance

This disclosure is made in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's (FTC) guidelines on endorsements and testimonials. We believe in full transparency about our affiliate relationships.
